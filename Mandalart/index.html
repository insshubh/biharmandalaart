About Bihar Mandalas

Mandala art has deep roots in Bihar's spiritual traditions, particularly in Buddhist practices. These intricate geometric patterns symbolize the universe and are used for meditation and spiritual guidance. Our generator incorporates motifs inspired by Bihar's ancient art forms found in temples and historical sites.
